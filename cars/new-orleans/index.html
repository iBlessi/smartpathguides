Buying a Car in New Orleans

Whether you're buying new or used, New Orleans, LA has numerous dealerships and private sellers to choose from. Being prepared with research and financing can save you thousands.

Car Buying Tips for New Orleans Residents

  • Get pre-approved for auto financing before visiting dealerships
  • Research fair market prices on KBB and Edmunds
  • Check vehicle history reports (Carfax/AutoCheck) for used cars
  • Get an independent mechanic inspection for used vehicles
  • Compare prices at multiple New Orleans dealerships before committing

Auto Financing in New Orleans

New Orleans car buyers should compare financing from dealerships, banks, and credit unions. Credit union rates in New Orleans are often 1-2% lower than dealership financing.

Your credit score significantly impacts your auto loan rate. Before shopping, check your credit report and consider improving your score for better terms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find the cheapest used cars in New Orleans?

Compare prices on AutoTrader, Cars.com, Facebook Marketplace, and local New Orleans dealerships. Private sellers often offer lower prices, but dealerships may include warranties.

What should I know about car registration in LA?

After purchasing a car in New Orleans, you must register it with the LA DMV, pay applicable taxes and fees, and obtain LA license plates within the required timeframe.
